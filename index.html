how to play

Its bingo! Get 5 in a row and you win. However, there are be additional rules you might want to play with such as:

  1. You can't mark a square until it acually happens in the episode (good for playing with people who have watched the show a bunch of times and know every episode by heart lol)
  2. If there is no bingo by the end of the episode, the person with the most squares marked wins
  3. You can close and open the app the reset your bingo sheet if you feel the squares you have don't apply well to the episode. However, you cannot do this if you've already marked a square or have reached ten minutes into the episode

You can find every possible bingo square on the list page. Check out the support section to find out how you can submit items to be added to the list!

support

If you have any questions or feedback about the app, or would like to submit items to be potential squares please follow the following steps:

  1. Go to the Barnaby Bingo repository issues page
  2. Click the new issue button near the top right of the page
  3. Add an appropriate title and type out your request
  4. Click the create button at the bottom right of the text form
